IPTV Doctor - IPTV Playlist Checker

IPTV Doctor is an open-source M3U and M3U8 playlist checker, HLS diagnostics tool, M3U cleaner, XMLTV EPG fixer, GitHub Actions health checker, and legal World Cup 2026 IPTV metadata exporter.

The project does not host streams, publish paid channel lists, or distribute Xtream credentials. Use it with your own legal IPTV playlist.
